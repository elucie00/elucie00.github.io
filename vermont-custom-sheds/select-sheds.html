Select Shed Info

Customized with the options you want

Don't need all the deluxe options included with a custom shed? Create your own Select Shed and choose the options you want to make a beautiful building.

It's structure is identical to a Custom shed with 5′ wide doors included. You can add a steep roof pitch, windows, vents, metal roof, overhangs, transom windows or upgraded siding. Add what you want, leave out the rest, save hundreds!

Select Shed Features:

Pressure treated 4×4 runners 1/2″ weather proof zip system sheathing
Pressure treated 2×6 floor joist 16″ on center 7/12 roof pitch
Floor covered with 3/4″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing 6″ to 8″ overhang / soffit detail
Walls framed with 2×4 spaced 16″ on center 30 yr warranty architectural shingles
Exterior covered with 5/8″ T1-11 siding Double doors with an opening up to 5′ wide included
Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters 16″ on center Regular duty ramp included
