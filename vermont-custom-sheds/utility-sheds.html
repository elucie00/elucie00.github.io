Utility Shed Info

Quality That Matters

Why settle for a drop-off-the-back-of-a-truck shed when you can have a site built shed for less money?

The most storage space for the least amount of money. My Utility sheds are built better than most factory made sheds for hundreds less. These attractive sheds are built with the lumber in the right places and will hold up to a Vermont winter.

No sales tax! Built on your site as a construction service. Why pay more?

Utility Shed Features:

4×4 pressure treated runners Pressure treated 2×6 floor joists spaced 24″ on center
5/8″ advanteck tonge and groove floor sheathing Walls framed 16″ on center
Exterior covered with 5/8″ T1-11 siding Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters 24″ on center
1/2″ osb roof sheathing 6/12 roof pitch
4″ to 6″ overhangs Architectural shingles
Double doors with an opening 4′ wide or 5′ wide on 10×16 or larger.
