Custom Shed Info

Quality That Matters

Custom Shed Features:

  • Pressure treated 4×4 runners
  • Pressure treated 2×6 floor joist 16″ on center
  • Floor covered with 3/4″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing
  • Walls framed with 2×4 spaced 16″ on center
  • Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters 16″ on center
  • 1/2″ weather proof zip system sheathing
  • 10″ to 12″ overhang / soffit detail
  • 30 yr warranty architecural shingles
  • Double doors with an opening up to 6′ wide included
  • Regular duty ramp included
  • Two windows of your choice included up to a 12×16 size.
    Three windows included on larger sizes.

Your Choice of:

  • Steep 10/12 or 12/12 roof pitch
  • T1-11, Board and Batten, Shiplap or Novelty siding
