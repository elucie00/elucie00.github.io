Select Shed Info:
Customized With the Options You Want

Don't need all the deluxe options included with a custom shed? Create your own Select Shed and choose the options you want to make a beautiful building.

It’s structure is identical to a Custom Shed with 5′ wide doors included. You can add a steep roof pitch, windows, vents, metal roof, overhangs, transom windows or upgraded siding.

Options & Upgrades →

Add what you want, leave out the rest, save hundreds!

Select Shed Included Features:
  • 6/12" roof pitch
  • 5/8″ T1-11 siding covering exterior
  • 6″ to 8″ overhang with soffit detail
  • 1/2″ weather proof zip system sheathing
  • Walls framed with 2×4 spaced 16″ on center
  • Pressure treated 2×6 floor joist spaced 16″ on center
  • Double doors with an opening up to 5′ wide
  • 3/4″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing cover on floor
  • Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters spaced 16″ on center
  • Architectural shingles with 30 year warranty
  • Regular duty ramp included
  • 4×4 pressure treated runners
