Why Settle For Less?

My prices are almost always lower for this type of construction.

After years of experience in home construction, I build my sheds the same way I would build a home. These are all basic construction standards and just because it’s a shed doesn’t mean it should be built using shortcuts or poor quality materials.

Some sheds are not built to last. A factory built shed is normally built using shortcuts that don’t show up right away. A high quality custom shed is a big investment and I’m sure you want yours to last a lifetime.

Have your investment built on your site and built to last!

The Vermont Custom Shed Difference
Built for Your Site

Built on site means built for YOUR site. Factory-built sheds use "One-size-fits-all" shortcuts that often cause the end product to degrade earlier than an onsite built shed. When it comes to building a quality structure, every site has its own unique requirements

In offering only site-built sheds, I can concentrate on building a quality product instead of having to think about trucking and hauling. I’m a carpenter, not a hauler, by building your product on site I can be the best carpenter I can be with no distractions

Keep It Local

My materials and lumber come from my local supplier and keep eveything within the New England economy. Most factory sheds are manufactured and brought in from hundreds of miles away.

Retain the economic benefits of your purchase in your own local economy. Keep New England business booming for generations to come

Instead of buying from another huge corporation with no interest in supporting local communities, buy from a local who knows the area and how to build to withstand Vermont conditions.
