Custom Shed Info:
Built On Site, Built Better, Built to Last

If you are looking for a high-end structure with great curb appeal that can increase your property value, choose a Custom Shed. Superior in every way to what’s available in the local shed market.

Compare my Custom Shed specs to any of the other shed companies, mine are built more like your home than a backyard shed.

Built on your site as a construction service, no sales tax!

Custom Shed Included Features:
Customizable Options:
Customize your shed the way you want. Your choice of roof pitch, siding, and windows from the options listed below are included in the base price of your Custom Shed
  • Steep 10/12" or 12/12" roof pitch
  • T1-11, Board and Batten, Shiplap or Novelty siding
  • Two windows of your choice. Three windows included on sheds larger than 12' × 16'
Other Included Features:
  • 10″ to 12″ overhang with soffit detail
  • 1/2″ weather-proof zip system sheathing
  • Walls framed with 2×4 spaced 16″ on center
  • Tall sidewalls
  • Double doors with an opening up to 6′ wide
  • 3 heavy duty hinges on each door
  • Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters spaced 16″ on center
  • Architectural shingles with 30 year warranty
  • Regular duty ramp included
  • 4×4 pressure treated runners
  • Pressure treated 2×6 floor joist spaced 16″ on center
  • 3/4″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing cover on floor
What You Want Built Where You Want

No matter where you go to buy a shed it will be an investment. That’s why it is important to look at all the details. If your goal is a building that’s going to last for many years go with a high quality, built on site shed that’s built to last.

Don’t settle for a lightweight dropped-off-the-back-of-a-truck shed. Have a custom shed built on site. Have the doors and windows where you want them. Have the shed built where you want it. Why buy something off of a shed sales yard where your choices are limited?
