We are really pleased with the 12′ x 16′ custom shed you built for us. We had looked at a lot of sheds from various sources including local vendors, big building supply box stores, and a number of online vendors. We could not find a design that worked for our beekeeping and honey processing business. We were happy to find that you would build to fit our needs.

Your communication from beginning to end was excellent. That plus your willingness to adapt the details made this an easy project for us.

That part was good but what really surprised us was the quality of your construction. The window frames are doubled, the swinging door frames are tripled. The ceiling plates are doubled. The doors are super well braced. The shingles are high quality. The ceiling joists are 2×6″ on 16″ centers not 2×4″.

Most of the kits we looked at had no ceiling at all and no collar ties or ceiling joists either but you were able to create an attic for us that we can use for beehive component storage! And those rugged ceiling joist add to the strength of the building. Your roof rafters are also 2×6″ on 16″ centers not the cheap 2x4s on 24″ centers like many of the pre-made sheds have. All of these details make a ruggedly built structure. The finish work and trim is precise and looks great. And the list of quality construction items goes on and on.

The cheaper built sheds from other sources don't even come close to the quality of your shed. This made the final cost a much better value than the online kits, or those box store sheds, or other pre-built sheds from local vendors.

In the end, we felt really good about being able to support a local person who supports a local family by doing top notch quality work! We made a good decision to have you build our shed.

Thank you!
