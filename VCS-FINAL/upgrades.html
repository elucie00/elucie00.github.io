Customize Your Shed

A few of the many different possible upgrades pictured here(from left to right, top to bottom):

Custom cedar siding, flower boxes, roof dormer with transom window, sliding doors, metal roof, and a large overhang

On this page you can choose the different options or upgrades you may want to add to your shed.

A well placed window or two can really change the look of your building. An added single door can make it easier to get in and out if you have large equipment stored closer to the double doors, and of course it’s hard to beat the look of board and batten, shiplap, or novelty siding.

Listed below are descriptions, examples, and pricing for some of the most commonly requested customizations. See examples of these upgrades in the Utility Shed, Select Shed, Custom Shed, and Sugar House galleries.
