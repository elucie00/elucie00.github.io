Customize Your Shed

On this page you can choose the different options or upgrades you may want to add to your shed.

A well placed window or two can really change the look of your building. An added single door can make it easier to get in and out if you have large equipment stored closer to the double doors, and of course it’s hard to beat the look of board and batten, shiplap, or novelty siding.

Listed below are descriptions, examples, and pricing for some of the most commonly requested customizations.

Want a recommendation, or looking for something unique not listed below?
