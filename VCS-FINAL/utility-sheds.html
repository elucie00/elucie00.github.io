Utility Shed Info:
Quality That Matters

Why settle for a drop-off-the-back-of-a-truck shed when you can have a site built shed for less money?

The most storage space for the least amount of money. My Utility Sheds are built better than most factory made sheds for hundreds less. These attractive sheds are built with the lumber in the right places and will hold up to a Vermont winter.

No sales tax! Built on your site as a construction service. Why pay more?

Utility Shed Included Features:
  • 6/12" roof pitch
  • 5/8″ T1-11 siding covering exterior
  • 4″ to 6″ overhang
  • 1/2″ OSB roof sheathing
  • Walls framed 16″ on center
  • Pressure treated 2×6 floor joists spaced 24″ on center
  • Double doors with an opening 4′ or 5′ wide on 10' × 16' or larger
  • 5/8″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing cover on floor
  • Roof framed with 2×8 ridgeboard and 2×6 rafters 24″ on center
  • Architectural shingles
  • BLANK
  • 4×4 pressure treated runners
