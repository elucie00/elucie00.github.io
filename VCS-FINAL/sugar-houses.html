Select vs Custom Sugar Houses

Whether you're making syrup, or just like the style, it's hard to go wrong with a classic New England Sugar House

Your choice between Select or Custom Sugar Houses. A select Sugar House includes all the structural components you need for an operational sugar house in the base price. Design your own by adding the options you want.

Ready to take your sugaring set-up to the next level? Invest in a Custom Sugar House

Sugar House Included Features:
Select Sugar Houses:
Choose the upgrades you want, leave out the rest. All of the features listed below are included in the base price of your Select Sugar House. Check our Options & Upgrades page for custom add-ons.
  • Functional cupola with one opening door
  • 5/8″ T1-11 siding covering exterior
  • 6/12" roof pitch
  • 5/8″ zip system roof sheathing
  • 2×6 rafters spaced 16″ on center with a 2×8 ridgeboard
  • 3/4″ Advantech tongue and groove sheathing cover on floor
  • Pressure treated 2×6 floor joist spaced 16″ on center
  • 4×4 pressure treated runners
  • Double doors up to 4′ wide
  • Architectural shingles with 30 year warranty
Custom Sugar Houses:
All of the same specs and features of the Select Sugar House, plus all of the upgrades below included in the base price of your Custom Sugar House.
  • Two opening barn sash windows on the cupola
  • Your choice of: T1-11, Board and Batten or Shiplap siding
  • 2 step fascia detail or steep roof pitch
  • 14″ x 14″ wood louvered vents
  • Two 2’ x 3′ barn sash windows
  • Metal roof
Function AND Form

The Select Sugar House includes all the basics for a sugaring building. A heavy duty structure built to last, double doors up to 4' wide, and of course a fully functional cupola with one opening door for steam ventilation.

See something you like, but don't want to break the bank? Add on any of the Options & Upgrades you'd find on a Custom Sugar House. Pay for what you need, leave out the rest!

The Custom Sugar House includes all the bells and whistles needed in a structure for a serious sugaring operation, or even just to increase your property value with a beautiful building. Your choice of siding, double doors up to 6' wide, and a fully functional cupola with TWO opening wooden barn sash windows allows you to customize your building to give your property a truly New England feel.
